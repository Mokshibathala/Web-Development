School Subjects and Topics

  1. Mathematics
    • Addition

      Addition is the process of combining two or more numbers to get a total.

    • Subtraction

      Subtraction means taking one number away from another.

    • Multiplication

      Multiplication is a way to add the same number several times quickly.

  2. Science
    • Plants

      Plants are living things that grow in the ground and need sunlight to live.

    • Animals

      Animals are living creatures that can move and need food to survive.

    • Water

      Water is a liquid that is essential for life. We drink it every day.

  3. English
    • Reading

      Reading helps us understand stories, information, and new words.

    • Writing

      Writing lets us share our thoughts using letters and sentences.

    • Grammar

      Grammar teaches us how to form correct sentences and use words properly.

  4. Art
    • Drawing

      Drawing is making pictures with pencils or crayons on paper.

    • Painting

      Painting uses colors and brushes to create beautiful art.

    • Craft

      Craft means making things with paper, glue, and other materials.
